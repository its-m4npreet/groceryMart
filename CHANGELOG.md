# Changelog

## [1.0.0] — 2026-03-01

### Initial Release
- Launch of the complete MERN stack Grocery E-Commerce System.

### Features
- **Admin Dashboard:** Full-featured admin panel for managing products, orders, users, and analytics.
- **Email Notifications:** Automated transactional and promotional emails for orders and updates.
- **WhatsApp Support:** Integrated WhatsApp messaging for customer support and notifications.
- **Responsive UI:** Modern, mobile-friendly design for seamless shopping on any device.
- **Secure Authentication:** JWT-based login, role-based access, and encrypted credentials for robust security.
